Service Overview

Our fiber optic division provides comprehensive services ranging from initial route planning and engineering to physical installation, splicing, and testing. We utilize state-of-the-art fusion Splicers and OTDR testing equipment to ensure minimal loss and maximum throughput.

Key Capabilities

  • Ribbon and single fiber fusion splicing
  • OTDR and Power Meter testing
  • Emergency restoration services
  • MDU/SFU FTTH installations
  • Aerial and underground construction
